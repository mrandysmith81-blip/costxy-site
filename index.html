Costxy Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 April 2026

Costxy ("the app") is a free expense-splitting app for Android. This privacy policy explains what information the app handles and why. The short version: Costxy stores everything on your device and does not send your data anywhere.

What information Costxy collects

Costxy does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. All the data you enter into the app — groups, members, expenses, settlements, notes, and dates — is saved to a local database on your device and stays there.

The app does not:

  • Require an account or sign-in
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Show advertisements
  • Send crash reports
  • Sell, rent, or share any personal information
  • Know your name, email, phone number, or any other identifying information

What stays on your device

When you use Costxy, the following is stored in a local SQLite database on your device:

  • Group names and the currency you picked for each group
  • Member names you add to groups
  • Expense titles, amounts, dates, and how each expense is split
  • Settlement records and any notes you attach to them
  • The app's own settings (such as your chosen theme)

None of this ever leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Export and import

Costxy includes a feature that lets you export all your data to a .json file. The file is created on your device and, when you share it, is sent only to the destination you pick (email, cloud storage, messaging app, and so on) using your device's native share sheet. Costxy itself does not upload anything.

Importing a .json file reads it from your device's local storage using the system file picker. Costxy does not download anything from the internet during this process.

Permissions

Costxy uses the following Android permissions:

  • Storage / files access — so the app can save exports and read imports you select. The app only reads or writes files you point it to via the system file picker or share sheet.

Third-party services

Costxy does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If you install Costxy from the Google Play Store, Google may collect information about the install and your device according to Google's own policies — this is outside Costxy's control and applies to every Android app.

Children's privacy

Costxy is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because the app does not collect any data, there is nothing to request or delete on a server.

Data deletion

Because all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • To delete a single group, expense, or settlement, use the delete controls inside the app.
  • To delete all Costxy data at once, uninstall the app. This removes the local database completely, and the data cannot be recovered by anyone afterwards.

If you previously exported a .json backup, that file remains wherever you sent it and is outside Costxy's control. You are responsible for deleting any exports you've shared.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at the same URL as this one, and the effective date at the top will be updated. Material changes will also be mentioned in the app's release notes on the Play Store.
